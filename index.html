The Platform

Streamlining access to specialist care across Canada.

Primary care providers navigate a complex referral landscape — variable wait times, inconsistent specialist information, and fragmented patient communication. Find My Doctor consolidates the entire referral pathway into a single, intelligent extension that operates natively within existing EMR environments, reducing administrative burden without disrupting established workflows.

⏱
Time-Intensive Referral Administration Manual coordination across directories, fax systems, and patient communication channels consumes significant clinical time per referral episode.
📊
Limited Specialist Visibility Sub-specialty focus, current availability, and proximity data are rarely accessible at the point of referral, complicating informed decision-making.
💬
Patient Communication Gaps Patients frequently lack timely updates on referral status, leading to unnecessary contact with both referring and receiving practices.
📠
Unstructured Specialist Responses Incoming fax communications — acceptances, rejections, and document requests — require manual review and follow-up with no automated routing.
Workflow

A referral workflow designed around the physician.

1
📋
Referral Initiated
Physician selects specialty and documents clinical indication within the existing OSCAR referral screen. Patient location is retrieved automatically.
2
⚡
Extension Activates
Find My Doctor reads EMR fields in real time via a lightweight DOM integration, triggering the intelligent matching engine without any manual input.
3
🎯
Specialists Ranked
A weighted algorithm surfaces the top matches by sub-specialty alignment, current availability, and proximity to the patient's home address.
4
✅
Referral Submitted
A single selection triggers simultaneous EMR documentation and patient SMS notification. The referral is complete in under two minutes.
🔌
Native EMR Integration
Deployed as a Chrome extension using a read-access DOM integration — the same proven approach as Cortico. Compatible with OSCAR Pro, OSCARwest, and OscarBC. No server-side configuration required.
🤖
Intelligent Specialist Matching
A configurable algorithm evaluates sub-specialty focus, real-time wait times, and geographic proximity to surface the most appropriate specialist for each referral context.
📱
Automated Patient Communication
Patients receive structured SMS notifications at each stage — referral submission, specialist confirmation, and a 24-hour appointment reminder — without any manual effort from clinic staff.
📠
Structured Fax Processing
Incoming specialist fax responses are automatically parsed and categorised. Acceptances, document requests, and rejections each trigger the appropriate downstream workflow.
🔄
Seamless Redirection
When a referral is not accepted, the next-ranked specialist is immediately presented with all patient context pre-populated. Redirection requires a single click with no data re-entry.
🔒
Privacy by Design
Patient data remains within the active browser session and is transmitted only upon explicit referral submission. The platform is designed for PIPEDA compliance from the ground up.

Automated handling at every stage of the referral cycle.

✅
Outcome · Accepted
Referral Confirmed
Upon specialist acceptance via fax, the EMR is automatically updated and the patient receives a structured appointment confirmation with full clinic details.
  • Appointment details recorded in EMR
  • Patient status updated to Booked
  • Confirmation SMS dispatched
  • Automated 24-hour reminder sent
📋
Outcome · Documentation Required
Additional Information Requested
When a specialist requires supplementary clinical documentation, the extension presents a parsed checklist and enables direct resubmission from within the EMR.
  • Document checklist extracted from fax
  • File attachment directly from EMR
  • Patient informed of pending status
  • One-click resubmission with attachments
🔄
Outcome · Not Accepted
Referral Redirected
If the initial referral is not accepted, the reason is logged, the patient is notified, and the next-ranked specialist is immediately presented for redirection.
  • Reason for non-acceptance documented
  • Patient notified without delay
  • Next-ranked match surfaced instantly
  • Redirection completed without re-entry
